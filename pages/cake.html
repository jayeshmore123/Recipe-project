Cake Recipe

Ingredients :

* 1 cup white sugar

* 1/2 cup unsalted butter

* 2 large eggs

* 2 tespoons vanilla extart

* 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

* 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder

* 1/2 cup milk

Preparation

* Gather all ingredients. preheat the oven to 350 degree f (175 degrees c). grease and flour a 9-inch square cake pan.

* cream sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl. Add eggs, one at time, beating briefly after addition. mix in vanilla.

* Combine flour and baking powder in a separte bowl. add to the wet ingredients and mix well. add milk and stir until smooth.

* Pour batter into the prepared cake pan.

* Bake in the preheated oven until the top springs back when lightly touched, 30 to 40 minutes.

* Remove from the oven and cool complaxity. frost and enjoy!
