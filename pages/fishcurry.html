Fish curry Recipe Instument

- 2 to 3 green cardamom

- 1/2 teaspoon mint leaves

- salt as required

- 3 green cardamom

- 2 onion

- 1 teaspoon turmeric

- 1 inch stick of cinnamon

- 2 tablespoon coriander leaves

- 600 gm chicken

- 1 tablespoon garam masala powder

- 1 teaspoon saffron

- 1 tablespoon bay leaf

- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

- 4 green chillies

- 1 tej patta (Indian bay leaf)

- 1 teaspoon red chilli powder




Fish curry Recipe Process

Heat 2 tablespoons of ghee in a deep-bottomed pan or pot.





Add the following whole spices and saute the spices for a few seconds or till they splutter and become aromatic.

-1 black cardamom

Then add Â½ cup of thinly sliced onions.





Saute for half a minute and keep aside.





Heat ghee in a thick bottomed pot or a deep pan. Add all the "whole spices" mentioned above
and fry till they crackle and become aromatic. Add the sliced onions and saute them till they turn
golden stirring often. Now add the ginger-garlic paste and chopped green chilies. Saute for a few
seconds or till the raw aroma of ginger-garlic goes away. Now add the nuts-seeds paste and curd.
Mix well. When you add the curd then either keep the heat to low or switch off the heat. Make sure
you beat the curd before you add and mix it quickly as soon as you add it.
