Sandwich Recipe

Ingredents :

* 4 slices bread (any)

* 3 tsps butter or ghee (optional)

* 1 tbsp oil

* 1/2 tsp garlic chopped or 1/2 tsp ginger garlic paste

* 1 pinch turmeric or haldi

* salt as needed

* 2 green chiles chopped or 1/2 tsp chil powder

* 1 cup mixed veggies heaped,(carrot, onion capsicum,corn,beans)

* 1 tomato large, finely chopped mashed (about half cup)

* 1/2 to 3/4 tsp garam masala or pav masala or any spice powder

* 2 tbsp coriander leaves

Instructions

Preparation :

* Add oil to the pan, add cumin and allow to splutter. saute garlic
until a nice aroma comes.

* Next add tomatoes and sprinkle salt. cover and cook until soft and
mushy. if using red chili powder add it now and saute for a minute

* Add the veggies and begin to saute on a medium to high flame until
half done or soft cooked to suit your taste. you can also cover and
cookn until soft.

* Next add masala powder and coriander leaves. stir and saute for a minute.
if needed add more salt. set this aside.
