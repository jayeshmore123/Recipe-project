Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

* 250 grams paneer (cubed to 1 by 1 inch pieces)

* 1/2 cup hung curd (or greek yogurt,refer notes)

* 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste or crushed ginger garlic

* 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon garam masala or tandoori masala

* 1/4 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fengreeek leaves)

* 1/8 teaspoon turmeric (optional)

* 1/4 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)

* 1 taspoon lemon juice (skip if your yogurt is sour)

* 1 teaspoon oil

* 1/2 small bell pepper (diced capsicum,optional)

* 1/2 small onion (diced & layers separted, optional)


For Masala Gravy

* 2 tablespoons oil (divided 1 tbsp. + 1 tbsp.)

* 1 cup onions cubed (2 medium)

* 1 1/2 cup tomatoes ripe chopped (3 large)

* 1/4 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)

* 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)

* 3/4 + 3/4 cup water (to blend + cook the sauce)
